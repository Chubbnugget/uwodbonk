### Quest Contents
A certain nobleman wants to commission a painter known as the ``Painter of Laughter'' to create one. However, since I've never seen the real thing, I just want to see a picture. Could you please find a painting that symbolizes that artist and introduce it to me? Ingrid must have been very knowledgeable about the paintings. Why don't you ask someone while drinking?
