### Quest Contents
This is a request from a scholar in this city. It seems like he's been doing research on ancient Mesopotamia lately. It seems that they want research materials about outstanding literary figures of ancient Mesopotamia and their works. You may have to go to the Beni Beach area, but if you're okay with that, please ask the client himself for more details.
