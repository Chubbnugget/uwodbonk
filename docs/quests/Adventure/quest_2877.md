### Quest Contents
You previously participated in a survey of stone monuments that seem to be related to Goguryeo, right? It seems that a scholar in Hangzhou would like to consult with you regarding the results of the survey. Apparently it's about the king of Goguryeo. I'm sure he's waiting for you, so go ahead and do it.
