### Quest Contents
Trade between Africa, Arabia, and India has long been centered on the ocean. This time, in order to ensure the safety of the trade route, they would like to request a detailed investigation. Expensive trade goods come and go, so if something were to run aground, it would be a huge loss. Speak to the port official in Aden and then investigate.
