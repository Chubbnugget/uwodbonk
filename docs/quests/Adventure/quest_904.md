### Quest Contents
A certain nobleman in Genoa recognizes your unusual discoveries and has asked you to compile a paper on them. They seem to be expecting something out of the ordinary, and are willing to spare no money. You should consult a scholar in this city about how to write your thesis. When it's completed, give it to the Genoese scholar.
