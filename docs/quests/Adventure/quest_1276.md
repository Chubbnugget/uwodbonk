### Quest Contents
It is said that the Hindu teachings that were born in this country have been passed down to the island of Java, which is located across the Indian Ocean. As proof of this, statues of Hindu gods can be found on the island of Java. Apparently, a scholar in this city is looking for that statue. First, gather information in Jakarta.
