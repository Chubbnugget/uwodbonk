### Quest Contents
This is an advanced training assignment for navigators who aspire to become military personnel. I want them to prove their ability by actually subduing bandits. The targets of subjugation are Ojak soldiers whose territory is east of Tripoli. East Tripoli can be reached from the city of Tripoli. Don't forget your disguise.
