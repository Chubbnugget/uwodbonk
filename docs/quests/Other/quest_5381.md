### Quest Contents
There is a theory that Atlantis existed in the Atlantic Ocean. Please research this theory in archives in London, Lisbon, and Seville and compile a report. You will be able to obtain the necessary information by reading archeology books. Please note that the order of the cities you visit does not matter.
