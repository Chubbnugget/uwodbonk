### Quest Contents
Conduct the Intermediate Commercial Course Completion Certification Examination. Once you complete the exam, you will be recognized as having completed this course. The challenge is the delivery of manufactured goods. If you're good at crafting, take a clean deck brush to the trading shopkeeper in Seville.
