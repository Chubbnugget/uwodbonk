### Quest Contents
This is a request to recruit instructors for graduates of the commercial course of a navigator training school. You have the skills needed to be an instructor. If you want to become a commerce instructor, get useful knowledge for trade from the commerce instructors at each home base, compile it into a paper, and give it to the commerce instructor in Seville. If you can submit your thesis, you will be issued a license.
