### Quest Contents
A huge pyramid towering over the land of Giza. It is said to be the tomb of King Khufu. A huge number of square stones were piled up by hand. The surface is covered with polished white marble and shines. During construction, housing for the workers was built around the building, and they were provided with food and beer.
