### Quest Contents
It's a perfect job for someone just starting out. I've received a request to deliver 5 barrels of duck meat to the town of Montpellier, which is a few days away by sea to the west of this city. The client is the master of a bar in Montpellier. Please purchase duck meat from the trading post in this city and deliver it to me. Please come and let me know once the delivery is complete.
