### Quest Contents
I hand over the imperial command to you. The goal is to secure water sources, which can be said to be the lifeline for long-term exploration. Food and water issues will arise if the field survey continues for a long time. In order to prevent this, we must first secure a water source. Immediately request the cooperation of the bar master in this town and begin the investigation.
