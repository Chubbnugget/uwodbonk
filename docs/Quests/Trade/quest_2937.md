### Quest Contents
It's a request from the merchant guild. The idea is to deliver food that will be eaten by soldiers during military expeditions. The food to be delivered is wine-steamed cod, and we have delivered 200 servings. The time limit is 30 days, which is strict, but please manage to complete it and hand it over to the departure point official in this town.
