### Quest Contents
This is an unusual request from the Navy. The purpose is to provide food for officers on military expeditions. The dish is bone-in mutton stewed in wine, and we delivered 100 servings. The delivery date is 30 days, which is quite strict, but please manage to get it all together and hand it over to the departure port official.
