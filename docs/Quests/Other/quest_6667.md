### Quest Contents
In order to increase immigration to the Chamber of Commerce District, there must be sufficient material aid for the journey. We are asking for the cooperation of the trading post owner in Gijon, so let's accumulate 20 barrels of wine there. Later, it will be delivered to the person who wants to immigrate.
