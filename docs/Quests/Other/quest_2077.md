### Quest Contents
Surveys into the Spice Islands often receive funding from nobles. Therefore, in order to demonstrate the significance and results of our research, we ask them to search for elegant flora and fauna that add interest to their lives. I have asked the guild master to do some preliminary research. Get the information and set sail immediately.
