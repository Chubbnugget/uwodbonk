This is a challenge to further develop the adjutant's ship doctor characteristics. Please head to Valparaiso with your lieutenant captain, taking care to ensure that the crew members do not experience any physical or mental problems. Upon arrival, please report the voyage status to the departure station. I hope you can arrive safely.
