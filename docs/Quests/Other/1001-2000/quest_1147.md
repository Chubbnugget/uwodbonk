### Quest Contents
Talented navigators, I present you with a great mission regarding advancing to the southern part of the New World. In unexplored territory, the fangs of creatures could pose a major threat to our country's expansion. Your mission is to investigate its existence. The arrangements will be communicated to you by the guild. I look forward to your excellent insight.
