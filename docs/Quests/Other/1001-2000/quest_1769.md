### Quest Contents
In order to advance to the west coast of the New World, investment from nobles and large merchants is required. So, I would like to deepen their interest in the new continent by reporting to them that there are strange animals there. The preliminary research is left to the adventurer guild, so listen to the details from the adventurer request intermediary and investigate the local animals.
