### Quest Contents
We have received a request to move to your city from a pharmacist named "Haru" who lives in Sakai. However, in order to prepare for the migration, it seems that they need 20 boxes of a fruit called noni. If you're looking for noni, you should be able to get it around Ternate... If you take it to Sakai, Yuki-san at the bar will take care of it for you.
