### Quest Contents
A calculation instrument said to have been used in the Roman Empire. Groove Soroban. Calculations are made by moving beads inserted into grooves on a plate made of metal such as bronze. It was long forgotten in the Nordic world, but it was improved and reintroduced by Pope Sylvester II around the 10th or 11th century.
