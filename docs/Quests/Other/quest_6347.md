It is said that Sostratos of Greece, who was involved in the construction of the Great Lighthouse, left his name for posterity by placing a device on the foundation stone of the Great Lighthouse. You may be able to find out more about anecdotes related to great relics that are listed as one of the Seven Wonders of the World in the Alexandria archives.
