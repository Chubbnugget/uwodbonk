### Quest Contents
A weapon craftsman named Hong Seong who lives around Hanyang has requested the delivery of 20 boxes of muskets. It seems that he wants to work hard on making new weapons in a new world, but before that, he wants to learn about European weapons. The delivery address is a workshop craftsman in Hanyang. Although you are far away, why not try collaborating?
