### Quest Contents
It is said that Hercules once saved the daughters of Atlas. In return, Atlas taught him the knowledge of astronomy. It is said that Hercules, who later achieved great feats, made offerings to the temple of Poseidon. Let's search for Hercules' offerings based on the information derived from the relic pieces.
