### Quest Contents
In order to build the solar ship, I would like you to investigate the volcano that is said to be the cause of the destruction of Atlantis. Based on the theory that Atlantis existed in the Aegean Sea, I would like you to investigate the area around the Balkan Peninsula. It would be a good idea to collect information at Salonika.
