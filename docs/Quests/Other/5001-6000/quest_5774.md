### Quest Contents
A trader named Sergio has requested the delivery of 20 barrels of ham. It seems that your company is preparing to move there, seeing your business in the development district as promising. The ham was probably a supply for the trip. They would like you to deliver it to the trading post owner in Las Palmas.
