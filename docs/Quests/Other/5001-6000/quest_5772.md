### Quest Contents
We have received a request from a woman named Leticia, a former Spanish naval officer, to deliver 50 pieces of sheet music for ship songs. After retiring from the military, he is thinking of immigrating to your commercial development district, and is currently making preparations for that purpose. Could you please procure it somewhere and give it to the representative in Kaitakugai?
