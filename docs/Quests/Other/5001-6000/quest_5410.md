### Quest Contents
When constructing the solar ship, we would like you to investigate the place where Atlantis is believed to have once existed. Based on the theory that Atlantis was on the Atlantic side of the Strait of Gibraltar, I would like you to investigate the coast near the Strait of Gibraltar. It would be a good idea to collect information in Ceuta.
