### Quest Contents
There was a strange report from the advance team, so an investigation was ordered. According to reports, in the area called Hinomoto, people sometimes go into the mountains and don't come out for a while. It's hard to believe, but they might be plotting to attack us. Listen to the person in the bar in your town and begin your investigation.
