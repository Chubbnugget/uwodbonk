### Quest Contents
I give you an edict. When connecting the Mediterranean Sea and the Red Sea, I would like you to conduct a detailed survey of the topography. It would be nice if there was something that could lead to a connection between the Mediterranean Sea and the Red Sea... First of all, please request the cooperation of the scholars in this city and proceed with the investigation. I wish you good luck!
