### Quest Contents
Edible incense called ``tai-tika'' was used in ancient China. It is said that Yang Guifei, who was said to be an unparalleled beauty and was compared to a peony flower, used it as a favorite. It is said that if you continue to take it, your body will begin to emit a fragrance.
