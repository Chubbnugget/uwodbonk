### Quest Contents
A red star located in the upper left of the constellation Orion, which is a first-magnitude star that makes up the Great Winter Triangle. In the constellation map, it is on the right shoulder of Orion. The name seems to come from the Arabic word meaning "hand of Jauser," but it is not known who exactly "Jauser" is.
