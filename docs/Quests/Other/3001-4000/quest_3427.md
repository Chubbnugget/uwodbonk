### Quest Contents
The Knights Templar, who were highly praised for their valor, were driven to ruin due to their vast wealth. However, it is said that the secret treasure that the Knights possessed was secretly hidden. Find the secret treasure of the Knights based on the map of the Knights' monk derived from the relic piece.
