### Quest Contents
Conduct the Advanced Commercial Course Completion Certification Examination. Once you complete the exam, you will be recognized as having completed this course. The task is to deliver 20 barrels of ward sold in Ragusa to a tool shop owner in Antwerp, two sewing tools to a workshop craftsman in Antwerp, and return here to report. Do this in 120 days
