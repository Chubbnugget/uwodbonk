### Quest Contents
This is a challenge to further develop the Sokoban characteristics of the lieutenant. This is a large order, but please transport 1,600 boxes of leather goods to the trading post in Santo Domingo with your lieutenant and captain within 30 days. You can pay in installments as long as it is within the time limit.
