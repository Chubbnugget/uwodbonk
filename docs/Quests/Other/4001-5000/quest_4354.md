### Quest Contents
Although they have succeeded in advancing to the southern part of the New World, their foundation has not yet been solidified, and it is unclear when foreign countries will reach out to them. Here we want to break free from others and show the world the power of our country. You should deliver food supplies to your comrades who are working hard to develop land northwest of Pernambuco. The accumulation area is dead wood.
