According to the information deciphered, Atlantis seems to have been destroyed by abnormal weather after suffering severe damage from a volcanic eruption and large waves. However, it seems that there is a treasure hidden in Labyrinth that shows the glory of life. Explore Labyrinths and find this treasure.
