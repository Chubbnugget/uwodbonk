### Quest Contents
Crete in ancient times had its own developed writing system, called hieroglyphs, which could be transcribed letter by letter onto clay tablets using seals. It seems that the seal is sleeping in Labyrintos. Let's search inside the labyrinth based on the information derived from the relic piece.
