### Quest Contents
An incense burner shaped like a frog. In Japan, warriors also practice incense, and this incense burner is said to have been owned by a military commander who was on the verge of unifying the country. There is a legend that the night before this warlord was assassinated during a rebellion, the frog in his incense burner croaked.
