### Quest Contents
I hand over the imperial command to you. The advance team reported that there was a volcano on an island in the southern part of the peninsula called Korea. If an eruption were to occur, it would cause damage to our country. Therefore, I order you to confirm the frequency and status of eruptions of that volcano. Please ask the departure port official for assistance.
