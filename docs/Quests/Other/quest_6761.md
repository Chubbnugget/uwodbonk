### Quest Contents
This is a challenge to further develop the adjutant's navigator characteristics. Try to find a route to Guam within 80 days with your lieutenant captain. If you report your voyage record to the local departure station within the time limit, you will complete the task. It will be a long voyage, but why not give it a try?
