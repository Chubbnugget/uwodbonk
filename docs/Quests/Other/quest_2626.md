### Quest Contents
It seems that the coachman staying in Cairo is looking for someone who seems to have the qualities of a caravan. The number of thieves has increased, and there may be a shortage of manpower. It seems like the condition is to be knowledgeable about livestock, but are you good at livestock trading? If you are confident, visit the coachman in Cairo.
