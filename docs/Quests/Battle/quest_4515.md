### Quest Contents
Have you ever been to Cape Verde? There are many small islands near the continent, and shipping routes are limited. This time's request is to subjugate pirates who appear on the few sea routes and collect tolls from trading ships. Just ask the port officials in Cape Verde and they'll tell you where they are.
