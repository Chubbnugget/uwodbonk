### Quest Contents
A tactical researcher is collecting information on battles at sea, would you like to help? They say they give you rewards based on the number of ships you destroy in designated areas. However, ships belonging to any country are not eligible. The designated area this time is off the coast of the Cape of Good Hope. Fight as much as you like and let me know.
