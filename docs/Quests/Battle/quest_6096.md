### Quest Contents
This is an advanced training assignment for navigators who aspire to become military personnel. I want them to prove their ability by actually subduing bandits. The target is an Algiers bandit whose territory is on the north coast of Africa. You can land on the north coast of Africa from the pier west of Algiers.
