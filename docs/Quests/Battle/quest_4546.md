### Quest Contents
There are two islands north of the city of Riga, both of which are owned by pirates. The island of Saaremaa in the south is the home base of the pirates, while the island of Huma in the north is a base where the pirates store stolen trade goods and food. This time, I want you to subjugate the fleet that protects it. Talk to the port official in this town.
