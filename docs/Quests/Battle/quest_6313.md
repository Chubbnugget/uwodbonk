### Quest Contents
This is an advanced training assignment for navigators who aspire to become military personnel. I want them to prove their ability by actually subduing bandits. The targets of subjugation are Syrian bandits whose territory is in the southern Mediterranean. There is a dock south of Benghazi. It's best to land from there.
