### Quest Contents
In order to be recognized as a full-fledged soldier, you must first participate in naval exercises. There is a training ship somewhere between this city and Genoa, which is east of this city. You can get there by leaving the port and heading east along the coast. Once you have successfully completed the exercise, please come back here and report.
