### Quest Contents
Conduct the Intermediate Maritime Course Completion Examination. Once you complete the exam, you will be recognized as having completed this course. If you can destroy 6 ships in an exercise with the designated fleet, you will pass. The fleet in question is called a "herd of fierce bulls with sharp horns." That's all the information given. Please contact Seville for information on the location.
