### Quest Contents
The sea between Britain and the island of Ireland, where Dublin is located, is called the Irish Sea, and it seems that unscrupulous poaching boats appear in that area. When they see another fishing boat, they fire their cannons and try to sink it. Is this malicious? The guild directly issued the subjugation order.
