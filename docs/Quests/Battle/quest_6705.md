### Quest Contents
Conduct the Intermediate Maritime Course Completion Examination. Once you complete the exam, you will be recognized as having completed this course. If you can destroy 6 ships in an exercise with the designated fleet, you will pass. The fleet in question is called "a group of lighthouses that are bright in the distance but dark at the base." That's all the information given. Get information about the location from Oporto.
