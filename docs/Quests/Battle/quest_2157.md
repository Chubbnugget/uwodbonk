### Quest Contents
The Tyrrhenian Sea south of Naples has long been a dangerous area for pirates, but the Ranchere, with its trident emblem, is the most feared. It seems that a large fleet of them is gathering in the Strait of Messina. So a plan of attack is being drawn up. Please check with the maritime guild master.
