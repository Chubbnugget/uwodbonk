### Quest Contents
This is an advanced training assignment for navigators who aspire to become military personnel. I want them to prove their ability by actually subduing bandits. The targets of subjugation are former Hafsid soldiers whose territory is west of Tunis. West Tunis can be reached from the city of Tunis. Don't forget your disguise.
