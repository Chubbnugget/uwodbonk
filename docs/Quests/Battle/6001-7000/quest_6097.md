### Quest Contents
This is an advanced training assignment for navigators who aspire to become military personnel. I want them to actually subjugate pirates and prove their abilities. The target of subjugation is the Makomer pirates who appear west of Sassari. They are good at shelling. Fighting at a distance is dangerous.
