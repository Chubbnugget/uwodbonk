### Quest Contents
As expected, he's a voyageur coming to this town, and he looks nice. That's the request. If you head southeast from here, you will find two islands. There's a town called Sao Tome on the southern island, but they say there are pirates in the nearby seas, so they want you to subdue them. First, talk to the bar master in Sao Tome.
