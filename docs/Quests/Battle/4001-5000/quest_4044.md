### Quest Contents
The Lieutenant Governor, Mr. Henry Morgan, has requested you by name. Apparently, the remnants of a group calling themselves the Orthodox Buccaneers are running rampant in the Western Caribbean. On behalf of the Lieutenant Governor, he wants us to subjugate about 20 of these ships and eliminate the root cause of the disaster.
