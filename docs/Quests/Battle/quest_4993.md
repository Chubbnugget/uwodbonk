### Quest Contents
A tactical researcher is collecting information on battles at sea, would you like to help? They say they give you rewards based on the number of ships you destroy in designated areas. However, ships belonging to any country are not eligible. The designated sea area this time is the Ligurian Sea. Fight as much as you like and then report back.
