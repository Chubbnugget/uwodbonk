### Quest Contents
Conduct the Intermediate Maritime Course Completion Examination. Once you complete the exam, you will be recognized as having completed this course. If you can destroy 6 ships in an exercise with the designated fleet, you will pass. The target fleet is a fleet that can easily handle any tactic. That's all the information given. Get information about the location in Ceuta.
