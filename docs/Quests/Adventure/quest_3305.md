### Quest Contents
You seem to be used to studying birds. At the moment, the guild is collecting information about creatures on the continent north of here at the request of the pioneer group. It seems that the port official in this city has heard a rumor that there is a bird with a distinctive striped pattern in the north, so could you please go check it out?
