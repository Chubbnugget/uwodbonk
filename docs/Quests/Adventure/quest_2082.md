### Quest Contents
Apparently there is a bird in the depths of Panama that the residents of the city call the ``mysterious bird.'' The bird is said to be a messenger of the gods with rainbow-colored feathers. Therefore, I would like to request you to investigate the mythical bird. Head to Panama and find out what this bird is.
