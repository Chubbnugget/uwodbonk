### Quest Contents
This is a request to recruit instructors for graduates of the adventure course at the navigator training school. You have the skills needed to be an instructor. If you want to become an adventure instructor, gather knowledge useful for adventures from the adventure instructors at each base, compile it into a paper, and give it to the adventure course instructor in Seville. If you can submit your thesis, you will be issued a license.
