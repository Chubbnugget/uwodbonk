### Quest Contents
This is a request from a certain noble person. It seems they want you to find flowers that can be used as ingredients for perfume. It seems like you're looking for a refreshing scent that reminds you of the end of summer.Do you have any idea? If not, why not try gathering information from the people who buy and sell tools in this town?
