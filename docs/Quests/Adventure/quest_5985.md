### Quest Contents
This is an intermediate training task for navigators who aspire to be adventurers. I hope you will take advantage of it and hone your skills. Please note that "biology skills" and "ecological research skills" are required. The content is a survey of flying squirrels that live in the northern region. Try gathering information at a bar in Oslo.
