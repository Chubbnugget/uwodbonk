### Quest Contents
Rumors have spread that there are many unknown animals in the Galapagos Islands, and scientists are eager to investigate. Would you like to try collecting information on the animals that live in the Galapagos Islands? Find out about the animals unique to the Galapagos Islands in Tumbes, the closest town to the islands.
