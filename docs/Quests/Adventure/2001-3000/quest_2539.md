### Quest Contents
There is a sweet called ``aigyokushi'' from the island of Taiwan, and a pastry chef who lives in this city has requested the raw materials for it. After hearing about it, he wanted to try making it himself. Well, if you listen to the story at a bar in the Anping area, you'll know right away. Can I ask you a favor?
