### Quest Contents
You're the adventurer who discovered the story Journey to the West, right? A request has been sent to you from a guild in Hangzhou. It seems that they are asking you to find the creature that Sagojo, who appears in Journey to the West, is based on. The client is a young man from Quanzhou. Go listen to the details.
