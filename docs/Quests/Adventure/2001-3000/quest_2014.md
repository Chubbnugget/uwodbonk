### Quest Contents
According to your research, there are people who immigrated to Aotearoa from Hawaiki in canoes, right? I want you to find out about that canoe. According to research, Hawaiki seems to be a very far place. Even if you're not a sailor, it's interesting to know what kind of performance canoes they used.
