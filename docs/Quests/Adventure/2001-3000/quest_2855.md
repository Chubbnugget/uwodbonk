### Quest Contents
You've received a request from a young man studying on Mt. Yuntai. They want me to borrow as accurate a history book as possible from a scholar in Hangzhou. It would be even better if it was grouped by dynasty. Will you accept it? In any case, even though we met when we were on the ship with Shiji... did you like him?
