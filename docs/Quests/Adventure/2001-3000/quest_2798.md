### Quest Contents
There seems to be a strange rumor going around among the caravan members surveying the inland areas. It is said that there is a fantasy land at the eastern end of the continent. Regarding this, Mehmet Sharuk has requested an investigation. Since it will be a difficult inland investigation, they would like to obtain the cooperation of an adventurer with excellent survival skills.
