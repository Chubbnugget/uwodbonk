### Quest Contents
This is a request from a certain lady. They want you to find flowers that are suitable for the garden of your mansion. First of all, I would like to find a flower that looks good and has a faint fragrance. Could you please help me find one? For these kinds of things, it might be a good idea to rely on experts. Wouldn't it be a good idea to ask the flower sellers in town?
