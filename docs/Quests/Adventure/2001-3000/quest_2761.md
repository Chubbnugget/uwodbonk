### Quest Contents
Did you find a poisonous snake called Hyapoda on Taiwan Island? There's an interesting story about that snake. Apparently, a race born between Hyappoda and humans has lived on the island of Taiwan since ancient times. A folklorist has requested an investigation into this matter. Could you please test it with fresh water?
