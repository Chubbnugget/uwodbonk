### Quest Contents
A request has come from a child named Zheng Ching in Anping. The request is for a navigator who is familiar with the area around Taiwan Island to find out more about his father. The client seems like a young child, but doesn't he know his father well? It's probably around the time you grew up looking at your parents' backs...
