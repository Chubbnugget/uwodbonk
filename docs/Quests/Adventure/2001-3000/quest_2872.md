### Quest Contents
You know Huang Gongwang, right? He is a landscape painter known as one of the four great masters of the late Edo period. According to rumors, he had a disciple. It seems that he also left behind some fine paintings. It seems that the official at the departure point in Macao is taking messages from clients, so please check the details of the request there first.
