### Quest Contents
A request from Hanyang's daughter. Apparently, one day, an unfamiliar stone statue suddenly appeared in the streets of Hanyang, and they were surprised. However, the next day it was groaning. I'm curious, so I asked you to find out what happened to that stone statue. For more details, please ask the person himself.
