It's a request from a certain lord. His territory seems to be filled with a disturbing atmosphere and he can't relax even at home. So, they wanted to place artwork that would bring a sense of peace to the room. You said you like art from South China, so please get some good information about South China. Is it better to gather information around Hangzhou?
