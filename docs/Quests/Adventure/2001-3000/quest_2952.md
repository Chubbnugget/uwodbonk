### Quest Contents
This is a request for a geographical survey from a surveyor. It seems they want you to gather information about the northern part of the continent. The surveyor seems to be looking for a river where he can move inland. The terrain in the north is complicated, so they want to rely on an experienced navigator like you. Is there anyone who knows a little bit about Santo Domingo?
