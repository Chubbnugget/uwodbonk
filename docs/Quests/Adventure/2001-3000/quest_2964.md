### Quest Contents
Looks like you discovered a big eel in the East. The guild is currently recruiting investigators for suspicious rumors, would you like to try it? In fact, there have been reports of sightings of eels with ferocious faces in various places. Although it looks like an eel, its face and teeth are unusually large. If you are interested, please gather information at the rest area.
