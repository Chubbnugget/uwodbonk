### Quest Contents
The Advanced Adventure Course Completion Certification Examination will be held. Once you complete the exam, you will be recognized as having completed this course. The exam requires ``exploration skills,'' ``archaeology skills,'' and ``lockpicking skills.'' The challenge will be presented by the adventure guild master in Seville. Go to him right away.
