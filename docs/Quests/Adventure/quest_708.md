### Quest Contents
The client this time is a certain nobleman in Spain. Apparently there is a land of mermaids somewhere in the world. I want you to look for it. It seems that the legends of mermaids vary depending on the location. I wonder if there really is a land of mermaids... If you want to talk about the sea, try asking at a bar.
