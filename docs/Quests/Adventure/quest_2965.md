### Quest Contents
It was a request from a certain nobleman. This is a person who is investing in the development of the city in a new land west of here, and apparently he heard rumors of a huge cave while surveying the land. They said they wanted to make sure that it wouldn't be an obstacle to the city's expansion. If you accept, please seek advice from the port official at Portopero.
