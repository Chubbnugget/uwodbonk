It is said that in the past, there was a military genius in the country around Tunis. It seems that he was a man who was like a god of war, with his tactics of underwhelming people. A scholar who studies the surrounding towns of ancient Egypt wants to find out about that general. Apparently he's a hero around Tunis, so ask someone.
