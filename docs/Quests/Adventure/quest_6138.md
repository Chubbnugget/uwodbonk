### Quest Contents
This is an advanced training assignment for navigators who aspire to be adventurers. Treat it as a test of your skill. The content is an investigation of ancient Greek statues said to have been excavated from the Delphi ruins. It would be good to hear from a workshop craftsman in Athens.
