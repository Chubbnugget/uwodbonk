### Quest Contents
There are reports that strange bones have been found recently. It has a body like a legendary dragon, but its limbs are said to be fin-shaped like a sea turtle. Find out if it's really a dragon or some other monster. It seems that Mogadishu is close to the discovery site, so there may be some information.
