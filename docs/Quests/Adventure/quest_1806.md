### Quest Contents
There is a passage in the New Testament that tells of John the Baptist being beheaded and executed. The reason for his execution was that Herodias' daughter asked for John's head as a reward for herself. Why did the girl ask for John's head? Could you please research this girl with a scholar from Alexandria?
