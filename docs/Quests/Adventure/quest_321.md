### Quest Contents
Do you know about Ramesses II of ancient Egypt? He was a pharaoh who reigned for a long time and built many temples. Recent research has uncovered records of a temple said to have been built by Ramses II, but apparently the location is not even known. Can't you find out somehow? Please consult a scholar in Alexandria.
