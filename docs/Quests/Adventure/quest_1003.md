### Quest Contents
Do you know anything about medieval Scandinavia? It's been quite eventful. Well, apparently there is a crown in Northern Europe that is said to bring domination. If that were true, there would be a lot of people who would want it. Would you like to look for it? First, try gathering information at a bar in Oslo.
