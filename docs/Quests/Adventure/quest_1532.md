### Quest Contents
Do you know Zheng He, the oriental navigator? Zheng He was an excellent navigator who made seven voyages to Africa. Apparently, he continued to participate in expeditions even when he was quite old. It is said that the journal recording Zheng He's voyage is hidden on this island of Sumatra. Please investigate in Palembang.
