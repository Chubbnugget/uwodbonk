### Quest Contents
It seems that the presence of cavalry archers had a big role in the Mongol Empire's success in expanding its territory. Being a nomad, he must have been skilled at horseback riding and using a bow. A workshop craftsman in this city has requested an investigation regarding that bow. Please ask the person himself for the details.
