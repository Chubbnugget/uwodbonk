### Quest Contents
A beautiful Virgin Mary is a common sight in recent painters' works. However, this was unthinkable in the past. Check out the paintings of the artists who pioneered it. The painter's name is Fra Filippo Lippi. Apparently, he married while still a monk and used his wife as the model for his painting. Let's meet at a bar in Venice
