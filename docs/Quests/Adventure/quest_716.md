### Quest Contents
A giant tree called the World Tree often appears in the legends of the northern countries. It's the tree that is the center of the world. This client is a scholar who has been compiling and researching descriptions of the giant tree. Read various books and look for sentences about the World Tree. You can see many books in the London archives.
