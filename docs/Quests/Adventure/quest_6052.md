### Quest Contents
Iconoclasm was an iconoclastic movement that was raging like a storm in the Eastern Roman Empire long ago, and some people want to know about its history. I can't give you the client's name, but could you look into it for me? If you go to Athens or Salonika, you might find someone who knows the details.
