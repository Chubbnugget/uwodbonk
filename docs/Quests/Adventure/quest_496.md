### Quest Contents
There are many heritage sites left in Egypt, but in order to analyze them, it will be necessary to understand the written language. This time it was a request from a scholar who studies ancient Egyptian writing. Please look for something that might be useful for researching ancient writing. First, you should talk to the client, a scholar in this city.
