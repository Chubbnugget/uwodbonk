### Quest Contents
Apparently there was an ancient civilization called Mesopotamia around Basra, and within that civilization was a dynasty called Babylonia. This is a request from a scholar who is researching that dynasty. Search for information near Basra that may be useful in unraveling the mysteries of Mesopotamian civilization.
