### Quest Contents
There is a town called Plymouth in the southwest of Britain. There's a narrow strip of water stretching from Dover to that town, and it's dangerous without accurate charts. This time's request is to survey the topography west of Plymouth. Check around the cape. Ask the Plymouth port official first.
