### Quest Contents
Apparently there are many rare animals on the island in the eastern part of the Indian Ocean. There is also a story that there is a bear the size of a human child on the island where Achin is located. The bar owner wants to know about this bear. If you have something to do over there, could you please check out the ecology of bears?
