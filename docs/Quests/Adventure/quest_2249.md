### Quest Contents
Hey...crabs are creatures that walk sideways, right? A certain scientist heard that there was a crab in the Galapagos Islands that could move forward and jump, and requested an ecological survey. There is a sailor in this town who has been to the Galapagos Islands, so ask him if he knows anything.
