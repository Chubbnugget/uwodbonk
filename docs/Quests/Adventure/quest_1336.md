### Quest Contents
If you go to any island east of India, you almost always have to pass through the eastern strait of Achin. If they captured the islands south of this strait, it would be as if they captured valuable trade goods from the eastern countries. So, the first thing to do is to conduct a local investigation. Head towards the town of Palembang on the island on the south side of the strait.
