### Quest Contents
Thank you for researching Oriental court cuisine. Thanks to this, verrine made with sea cucumber is very popular in Marseille. But lately, there's been a rumor among chefs that live fish can come out of sea cucumbers... Could you please investigate the identity of the fish? For now, please ask the fish seller in town.
