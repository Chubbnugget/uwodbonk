### Quest Contents
This is a request from a certain scholar. If shacao and palm oil are made for ritual purposes, there must be a place where rituals are held...and he has a hypothesis that he would like to confirm. Did you see a place like that when you found the ruins? Does the owner of the rest stop in Guam know anything?
