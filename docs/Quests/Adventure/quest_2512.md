### Quest Contents
Apparently, a Tamsui sailor spotted a huge marine creature between Tamsui and Nagasaki. It looks like he's got a lot of injuries. A scholar from Anping hears this and is worried that he might die, so he requests confirmation of the creature. If you are confident in biology, ask the discoverer's sailor for details.
