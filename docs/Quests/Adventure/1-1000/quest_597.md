### Quest Contents
In the mythology of the Yoruba people who live east of here, there are 1,700 gods. It is said that among them there is a god who brings conflict to humans. Could you please research about this god? If you go to Sao Tome, you'll probably find people who know the details. You can ask at a bar in Sao Tome.
