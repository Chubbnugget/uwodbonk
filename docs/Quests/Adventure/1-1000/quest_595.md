### Quest Contents
Did you know that in the 14th century, there was an outbreak of the Black Death? A scholar who compiles the history has asked me to investigate the Black Sea, but what do you think? Apparently, it's believed that it spread by boat from the Black Sea, and they want you to go on a boat and investigate. An Athenian scholar would know something.
