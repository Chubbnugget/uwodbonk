### Quest Contents
Do you know what happened to Alexander the Great's country after his death? It was divided into several countries, one of which was the Kingdom of Syria. It's a country that stretches from Asia Minor to India, and a scholar from Alexandria is looking for a statue of its founder. Please listen to the story.
