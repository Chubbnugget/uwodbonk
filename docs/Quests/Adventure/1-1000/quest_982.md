### Quest Contents
Who comes to mind when you think of the worst tyrant in history? I'm sure there are quite a lot of people who say they are Nero. Nero built a palace called the Golden Palace. After his death, it was demolished and the Colosseum was built. He wants to find the bronze statue that was there. Just ask the Neapolitan scholars.
