### Quest Contents
Did you know that Istanbul and the Anatolian Peninsula were once under the control of the Roman Empire? In the early days of the empire, Christianity was prohibited, but it is said that missionary activities were relatively active in that area. Why? We have received requests from scholars regarding this matter. Check it out in Athens.
