### Quest Contents
There is a story that during the time of ancient Egypt, there was a powerful nation called the Hittites. Was it suddenly destroyed by a mysterious tribe that appeared from the sea? This time's job is to research an ancient race that is said to have come from that sea. Please consult the Athenian scholars.
