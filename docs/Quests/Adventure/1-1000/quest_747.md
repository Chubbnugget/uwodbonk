### Quest Contents
In its final stages, ancient Rome was divided into east and west. Do you know how Western Rome fell? The story is that it was destroyed by mercenaries... There's a request to find out about the mercenary that was destroyed. It seems like he would like to have some mementos. Looks like a Genoese scholar might have a clue.
