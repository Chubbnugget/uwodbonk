### Quest Contents
Have you been to the islands far to the west yet? It seems to be a paradise of eternal summer in the coral sea. However, it seems that pirates often appear in search of the rich island. So the Navy is trying to make accurate maps to maintain public order. This time, check out the islands to the northwest of Jamaica.
