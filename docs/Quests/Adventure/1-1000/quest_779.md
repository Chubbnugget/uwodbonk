### Quest Contents
Do you know about Ibn Battuta's ``A Journey to the Three Continents''? It's a memoir from an Islamic traveler. This is a request to look into the original text. There's a traveler in Tunis named Leo Africanus, and I'm sure he'll know the details. Please listen to the story.
