### Quest Contents
Someone said that it was the era of the Five Wise Emperors as ``the happiest era in human history,'' but apparently the emperors that followed were miserable. It was a request from a scholar to investigate the emperors who are said to be tyrants after the Five Wise Emperors. Gather information around Naples.
