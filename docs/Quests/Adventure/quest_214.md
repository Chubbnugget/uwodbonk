### Quest Contents
You probably know the three great knightly orders of the Crusades. Among them, the Teutonic Knights are better known for their activities in eastern Germany than for their original activities. The request was to search for the belongings of the Teutonic Knights. If you ask around in a bar in Lübeck, you'll probably know something.
