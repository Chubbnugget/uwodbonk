### Quest Contents
This is a request to investigate Mr. Dürer's paintings. There was a rumor that he had created some wonderful paintings even though no orders had been given to him. A nobleman wants to buy it. Please go to Lübeck and find out what kind of painting it is. Oh, also, could you please confirm whether it is possible to purchase it?
