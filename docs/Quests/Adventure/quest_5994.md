### Quest Contents
This is an intermediate training task for navigators who aspire to be adventurers. I hope you will take advantage of it and hone your skills. Please note that "geography skills" and "visual recognition skills" are required. The content is a survey of the area around the cape west of Plymouth. Please speak to the port official at Plymouth.
