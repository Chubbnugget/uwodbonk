### Quest Contents
You seem to be knowledgeable about geography. I have a job that I would like to ask you to do. There's a rumor that you can constantly hear thunder in the forest, even when there's no storm. My request is to find out its true identity. The client is a town official from Sofala. Could you meet me in person and ask me the details?
