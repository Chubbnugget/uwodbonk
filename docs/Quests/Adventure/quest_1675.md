### Quest Contents
Thanks to you discovering Aotearoa, information about that island is starting to come in. It seems that the inhabitants of the island have a unique culture that is not found on other islands. Dancing seems to be their specialty. Therefore, I would like you to investigate items related to this. Go directly to Whanganui.
