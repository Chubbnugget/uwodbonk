### Quest Contents
You've been asked to investigate by a scholar of ancient Rome. At that time, those who saved the lives of Roman citizens were presented with a crown. It was an honor worth more than anything else. It seems that they would like an investigation into the crown and honor. A Neapolitan scholar seems to know the details.
