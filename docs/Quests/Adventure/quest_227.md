### Quest Contents
This is a request from a wealthy man. It seems that the treasures of Troy, which are said to have been destroyed in the Trojan War, are hidden somewhere. He wants to know what it's like. It was said that the Trojan War was a mythical story, but now that you've found the ruins of Troy, maybe the war really happened. Gather information at Salonica.
