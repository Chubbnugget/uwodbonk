### Quest Contents
It was a request from a shoemaker. There are rumors that there are lizards that can easily cross the scorching desert. It seems that they would like to study its ecology and develop shoes with high insulation properties. Nzinga Nkwa of Saint-Georges should know the detailed ecology.
