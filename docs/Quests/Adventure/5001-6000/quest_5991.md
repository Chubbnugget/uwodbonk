### Quest Contents
The Advanced Adventure Course Completion Certification Examination will be held. Once you complete the exam, you will be recognized as having completed this course. Please note that the exam requires ``ecological research skills'' and ``biological skills.'' The challenge will be presented by the adventure guild master in Seville. Go to him right away.
