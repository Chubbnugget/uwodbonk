### Quest Contents
Intermediate adventure course completion certification exam will be held. Once you complete the exam, you will be recognized as having completed this course. Please note that the exam requires ``archaeological skills'' and ``exploration skills.'' The challenge will be presented by the adventurer guild master in Seville. Go to him right away.
