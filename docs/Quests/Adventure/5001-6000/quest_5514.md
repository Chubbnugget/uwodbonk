### Quest Contents
You've discovered the Malay Peninsula, and we've got a perfect request for you. Apparently there is a strangely shaped island on the west side of the Malay Peninsula. There's talk of an island that looks like it's going to collapse but doesn't, but what exactly is that? If you'd like, please talk to a young man in this town.
