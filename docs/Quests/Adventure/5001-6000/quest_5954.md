### Quest Contents
Conduct the Beginner Adventure Course Completion Certification Examination. Once you complete the exam, you will be recognized as having completed this course. The test requires ``ecological research skills,'' but you can also use ``capture nets'' that can be purchased at the tool shop in Sagres. Prepare one of these and head to the scholar in the library in Lisbon.
