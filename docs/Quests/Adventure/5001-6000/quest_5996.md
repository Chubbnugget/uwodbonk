### Quest Contents
This is an intermediate training task for navigators who aspire to be adventurers. I hope you will take advantage of it and hone your skills. Please note that "geography skills" and "visual recognition skills" are required. The mission is to investigate an island to the west of Palma. Gather information at the bar in Parma.
