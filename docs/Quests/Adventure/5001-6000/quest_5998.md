### Quest Contents
This is an intermediate training task for navigators who aspire to be adventurers. I hope you will take advantage of it and hone your skills. Please note that "archaeology skills" and "exploration skills" are required. The story is an investigation into an irregular crusade led by a monk. Try gathering information at a bar in London.
