### Quest Contents
This is a beginner's training task for aspiring adventurers. The content is the discovery of the "Touhou Travelogue." ``Exploration Skill'' and ``Observation Skill'' are required, but ``Dowsing Rod'' and ``Artist's Observation Skill'', which can be purchased at the tool shop in Sagres, can also be used instead. When you're ready, speak to the scholar in the library in Lisbon.
