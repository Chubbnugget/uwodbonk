### Quest Contents
This was a request from a certain European explorer. While investigating the forest on Celebes Island, he apparently came across a beautiful butterfly perched on a tree. Apparently, he can't forget this butterfly even after returning to Europe. Can you please find this guy? Apparently they have told the story to the owner of the rest area in Makassar.
