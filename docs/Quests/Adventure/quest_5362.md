### Quest Contents
This is a request from a scholar in this city. In ancient Rome, the temple of the god of agriculture was apparently highly valued. He wants us to investigate the reason for this and whether the temple still exists. A scholar in Seville seems to know a lot about this kind of thing, so why not go and listen to him?
