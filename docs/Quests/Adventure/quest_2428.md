### Quest Contents
Do you know the calcio that is popular in this town? A ball game in which you use your feet and hands to carry a ball to a designated location. It seems that a certain aristocratic boy is so obsessed with this that he has people researching whether there are other ball games like this in the world. The port official knows the details, so go there.
