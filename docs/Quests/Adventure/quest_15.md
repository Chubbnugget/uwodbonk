This time, we will be investigating the ancient civilizations of the Anatolian Peninsula. This is a place where many ethnic groups and countries have had control over the area. It seems like research can't keep up. You say you need artifacts for your research, but first, talk to a scholar about what kind of artifacts they are and where you can find them.
