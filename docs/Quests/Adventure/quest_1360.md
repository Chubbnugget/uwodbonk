### Quest Contents
Do you know about Tombodama? It is a spherical piece of glass with a hole cut through it so that a string can be passed through. It has been made in India and Mesopotamia for a long time, but Javanese also seems to have excellent Tombo-dama. The merchant I spoke to wants to know the details. Please try to gather information in Jakarta.
