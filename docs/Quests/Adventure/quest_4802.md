### Quest Contents
A sailor in Acapulco said he saw a creature jump out of the sea, fly into the air, and then dive back into the sea. However, that person is not a bird. Isn't it true? So, a request has come to the guild to investigate this creature. A good place to start is to listen to the story of the sailor in question.
