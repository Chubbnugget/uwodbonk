### Quest Contents
Have you ever investigated crocodiles? Crocodiles have the impression of being ferocious and attacking people. In fact, they also live on land on the continent southeast of here. For the sake of the safety of voyagers heading to the continent, I want you to investigate it. Ask the departure station official about the investigation procedure.
