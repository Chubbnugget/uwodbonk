### Quest Contents
Sacrificial rituals are common in this area. Moreover, this custom has been going on for a long time. It seems that a certain scholar has become interested in the history of sacrifices, so this time we will be investigating it. Go back as far as you can and do some research! Looks like you know who the gatekeeper of Cusco is.
