### Quest Contents
This is a request from a scholar. The Ayutthaya dynasty flourished at the base of the Malay Peninsula, and it seems that this dynasty grew in size due to the rivers flowing nearby. It seems that they want to know what it means to be indebted to the river. Please check Malacca first.
