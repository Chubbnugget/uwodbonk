### Quest Contents
We will investigate why the people of the Inca Empire are becoming more wary of us. If you can get the cooperation of a merchant with high social skills, you will be able to hear from the gatekeeper of Cusco. There is a risk that a battle will ensue as they are being guarded. Let's enlist the help of military personnel.
