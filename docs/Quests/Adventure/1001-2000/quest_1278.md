### Quest Contents
Do you know about the Mongol Empire? It is a country of nomadic tribes that lived in the inland grasslands. King Kublai once invaded the island of Java with a large fleet in an attempt to secure maritime trade routes. I want you to find out about Kublai. It would be a good idea to ask around town.
