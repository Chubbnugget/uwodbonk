### Quest Contents
It is said that in the depths of Pegu, there was once a prosperous land called Pagan. Apparently there was a dynasty that lasted for a very long time. If you're as skilled as you are, wouldn't it be possible to unearth artifacts from the early days of that dynasty? First, try gathering information around Calcutta.
