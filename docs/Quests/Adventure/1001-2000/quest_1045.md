### Quest Contents
When it comes to paintings, there are trends from time to time. In France in the early 15th century, there were many religious paintings made of gold. I would like you to look for one of the paintings, ``The Martyrdom of Saint Denis,'' and investigate it. Try gathering information at a bar in Nantes.
