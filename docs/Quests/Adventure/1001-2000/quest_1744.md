### Quest Contents
Great information has arrived! It seems that on the southern continent, there are axes whose blades are made of gemstones. Moreover, it seems that it is actually being used. If this story is true, wouldn't that mean there are enough gems to sweep up and throw away? First, try interviewing Kakadu on the north coast of the southern continent.
