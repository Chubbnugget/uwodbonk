### Quest Contents
I am requesting you to conduct a confidential investigation. I would like you to explore the locations of the islands scattered in the Pacific Ocean. In order to connect a sea route from here to the New World, it is essential to replenish supplies at small islands in the Pacific Ocean. Anyone who can figure out the location of this islet will gain great wealth! Please try interviewing in Manila first.
