### Quest Contents
Lately, there's been some strange ghost activity on the outskirts of Merida... Apparently, you hear voices searching for people and see strange lights every night. It may be a strange job to leave to you, an adventurer, but maybe that light is the shine of a treasure? Please! Please listen to the story in the city of Merida.
