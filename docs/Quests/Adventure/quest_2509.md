### Quest Contents
At the request of a certain explorer, there is a bird he wants me to find on the island of Taiwan. It has two long tails that flutter when it flies. They say they just saw it flying around the city and couldn't make out any other characteristics... If you accept, I'll ask you to gather information around Anping.
