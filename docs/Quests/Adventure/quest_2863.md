### Quest Contents
This area has had many earthquakes since ancient times, and the city has even been destroyed by tsunamis. It seems that there are many earthquakes in the southern China region, and the epicenter has been detected for a long time. Apparently a certain sailor heard about it from Matteo Ricci in Macao. A scholar wants to know the details, so could you please look into it?
