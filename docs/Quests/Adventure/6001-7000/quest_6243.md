### Quest Contents
As an adventurer, it's only natural to learn the basics of how to survive a naval battle. A Portuguese Navy combat training ship is waiting somewhere between Oporto, the neighboring city to the north, and Lisbon, so it would be a good idea to pick it up. Even though it's an exercise, it's a real gun, so don't let your guard down!
