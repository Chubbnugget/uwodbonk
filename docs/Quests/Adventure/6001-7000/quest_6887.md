### Quest Contents
There are a wide variety of currencies in circulation in the world, both now and in the past. It is said that among the coins of the East, there were circular coins that had an influence on surrounding countries as well. We've received a request from a scholar in this city to investigate the ancient coins. Please meet me in person and ask me the details.
