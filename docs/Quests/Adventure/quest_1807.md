### Quest Contents
Do you know a painter named Tintoretto who is currently on sale? It seems that the painting depicting St. Mark's last moments is very popular. So, as a request from the guild, would you be able to find out what that painting looks like? It seems that a scholar in Naples is doing some research, so please listen to him.
