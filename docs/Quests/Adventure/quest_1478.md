### Quest Contents
It is said that there is a Buddhist statue related to Vishnu in a town located on a tributary of the Chao Phraya River. It is said to be so beautiful that Vishnu helped build the Buddha statue. The people at the temple want you to investigate the statue. First, please investigate downstream in Lopburi.
