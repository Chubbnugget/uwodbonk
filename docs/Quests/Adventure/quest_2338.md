### Quest Contents
Do you know what waka is? This is a song that has been passed down in this country since ancient times, and is composed of five and seven tones. There are many books that collect waka poems, but there are many enthusiasts who always want to read the oldest ones. I want to ask you to explore. For starters, ask a scholar in Sakai.
