### Quest Contents
It seems that in the northern island nation of the Spice Islands, there is a person who is worshiped as the god of learning. A scholar in this city wants to find out about that person. A scholar alone won't be able to cross the sea, so please lend me a hand. For more details, please ask the scholar.
