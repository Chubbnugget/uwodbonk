### Quest Contents
This was a request from a sailor who used to stay here. Apparently something like a drum was placed in Hanyang. Apparently, when he tried to hit it, a resident stopped him. However, the resident didn't know why hitting was not allowed. He's curious and wants you to go to Hanyang and find out why.
