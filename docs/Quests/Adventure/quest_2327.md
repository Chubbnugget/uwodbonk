### Quest Contents
The island country north of Manila that you went to seems to be characterized by its seasonal nature. A young writer in London seemed to be interested in this, so he named you and asked you to investigate. There are probably some similarities and differences between island nations. ...Isn't it interesting? Let's check out Nagasaki first.
