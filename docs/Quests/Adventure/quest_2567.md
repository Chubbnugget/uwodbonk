### Quest Contents
You know a book called Touhou Kenroku, right? There is a rumor that there is a painting depicting the city of gold in the ``Land of Gold'' written there. So the guild has a request. Please confirm the truth of this rumor. Nagasaki is the port that serves as the entrance to the "Land of Gold." You should check there first.
