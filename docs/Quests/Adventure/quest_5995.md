### Quest Contents
This is an intermediate training task for navigators who aspire to be adventurers. The content is an investigation of the ancient town of Carthage, which requires ``archaeology skills'' and ``visual recognition skills.'' Gather information at a rest stop in Tunis. In order to enter Tunis, a "disguise" is required. If you're not confident in your disguise, you can buy uniforms and hats at the tool shop here.
