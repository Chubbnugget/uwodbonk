### Quest Contents
It was a request from a royal servant. In Southern China, court cuisine made from soft and chewy sea creatures is said to be famous. It seems that the chefs who heard the rumors are determined not to be defeated by Oriental cuisine. For more details, please ask the guard at the palace.
