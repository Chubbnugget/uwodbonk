### Description
The capital of the Kingdom of Malacca, a country of Malays whose official religion is Islam. The Kingdom of Malacca originated from Parameswara, a prince of the Srivijaya Kingdom, who fled from the invasion of the Majapahit Kingdom and founded the country here. Cultural area: Southeast Asia Suburbs: Northwest of Malacca
