### Description
A city in northeastern Africa. Facing the Red Sea, it is also known as the "Pearl of the Red Sea." It is an area with a very old history, and there are many remnants of early Christianity. In addition to agricultural products, mainly beans and grains, coffee, which is native to this region, is distributed. Suburbs: Southwest Massawa Cultural area: Arab
